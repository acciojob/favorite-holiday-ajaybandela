Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean, known for its white sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and abundance of marine life. It is a popular destination for honeymooners, families, and water sports enthusiasts.

  1. Snorkeling and Diving: Explore the colorful coral reefs and swim alongside turtles, manta rays, and whale sharks.
  2. Island Hopping: Visit different islands and experience the local culture, cuisine, and way of life.
  3. Spa and Wellness: Indulge in a relaxing spa treatment or yoga session in a serene setting.
  4. Sunset Cruise: Watch the sun set over the Indian Ocean while sipping on a cocktail and enjoying a delicious dinner.
  5. Water Sports: Try your hand at jet skiing, parasailing, or windsurfing for an adrenaline rush.
  • Hedhikaa: Maldivian snacks such as fish cakes, samosas, and roshi (flatbread).
  • Garudhiya: A fish broth served with rice, lime, and chili.
  • Mas huni: A breakfast dish made with shredded smoked tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Bis keemiya: Fried pastries filled with spiced meat or vegetables.
  • Fihunu mas: Grilled fish marinated with chili, garlic, and ginger.
